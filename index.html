- Artist Introduction -


The current themes are mostly about uncertainty and fear of the unknown in relation
to one's surroundings and future situation. I believe that creation is also a process of healing,
and that there is a hidden ugliness, dirtiness and mystery in the fables,
trying to discover the detached, unpleasant part of the language that exists in the vision.

In the future, I would like to continue to focus on how the emotions and aesthetics of rationality,
restraint and pinnacle are integrated into the story through the brushstroke.
The focus is on using this contradictory personal feeling as an undercurrent for the creation
of a small number of human beings and events.
Some of these events are sudden and some have always been present in human life,
yet forgotten and ignored. At the same time, it is also expected that more different
ways of presentation will emerge in conjunction with other fields of art,
hoping that the hand of artistic practice will bring difficult and obscure
theories closer to life, rather than keep them on the shelf,
and perhaps bring an answer to many who are confused.

By creating works through multiple-media, I hope to combine traditional art creation
tools with creative programming in the future.
